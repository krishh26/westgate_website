    Subscription fees can be paid via bank transfer, credit card, or other accepted methods, with payment due annually upon subscription renewal.

  • 7. Can Suppliers request a breakdown of fees or an invoice for their subscription package?

    Unfortunately, no during first year. But YES, for the second and later years. For clarity, you can pay in 2 instalments from second year at 1 and 7th month.
