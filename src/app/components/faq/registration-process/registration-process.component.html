FAQ’s - Registration Process

  • 1. How do I register as a supplier with Westgate?

    To register as a supplier with Westgate, simply fill out the registration form provided on our website.

  • 2. What information do I need to provide during the registration process?

    You will need to provide basic personal information such as your name, designation, email id, and mobile number, along with company details and subscription preferences.

  • 3. Is there any fee for registering as a supplier with Westgate?

    The registration Process will be completed only when a package has been selected by the supplier. No Services will not be at free cost. There are 4 subscription packages which any supplier can choose to complete the Registration with us. Please refer to “Our Services” more information on Fee Structure.

  • 4. Can I bring all my branches under one registration?

    Yes, you can register your multiple branches under the same account.

  • 5. What happens after I submit my registration form?

    After submitting your registration form, our team will review your information and contact you to complete the registration process.

  • 6. What should I do if I encounter any issues during the registration process?

    If you encounter any issues during the registration process, please contact our customer support team [support@westgateithub.com or +917781978685] for assistance.

  • 7. Can I register as a supplier if my company is a startup or newly established?

    We welcome startups and newly established companies as long as they can justify their recent works as, it’s Westgate’s Policy to engage with any supplier only when they have case study recorded.
