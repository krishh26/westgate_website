    Public sector projects are government-funded initiatives. They include projects in areas such as healthcare, education, transportation, and social welfare.

    Private sector projects are initiatives undertaken by privately owned businesses or organizations for commercial purposes. These can include product development, marketing campaigns, and other ventures aimed at generating profit in the competitive market.

    Yes, Westgate offers ongoing support to Suppliers throughout the project lifecycle, including Feasibility Study, Bid Tracking, Bid Review and/or Writing Support from UK Experts and support to the Supplier once the Bid is Awarded in Project Co-ordination with Client.

    The feasibility study by Westgate evaluates the technical, financial, and operational aspects of potential projects. Its purpose is to determine if a project is viable and aligns with the supplier's capabilities, ensuring successful project outcomes.
