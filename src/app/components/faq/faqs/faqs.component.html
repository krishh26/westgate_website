Yes, Indian IT firms can choose from various working models such as subcontracting only, hybrid, on-site, and resource sharing, based on their preferences and project requirements.

The subcontracting-only model involves Indian IT firms subcontracting projects to their own team in India, while the hybrid model combines subcontracting and direct involvement based on project needs.

In the on-site model, Indian IT firms are directly involved in on-site project management and execution, providing hands-on support and expertise.

The resource-sharing model enables Indian IT firms to generate recurring income by sharing expert resources with UK Suppliers for specific projects.

The eligibility criteria include being a registered IT firm in India, having a business track record with case studies of previous projects, and agreeing to pay the fee/commission for accessing Westgate's services.
