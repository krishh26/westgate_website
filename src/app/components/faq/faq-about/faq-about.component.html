FAQ’s - About Us

  • 1. About Westgate?

    Westgate is a leading consultancy firm specializing in connecting Indian IT companies with opportunities in the UK IT sector.

  • 2. When was Westgate established?

    Westgate was established in 2022 with the aim of bridging the gap between Indian IT firms and UK projects.

  • 3. About Spectrum?

    Spectrum IT Hub Limited is a UK-based company specializing in providing IT solutions and services. With a strong presence in the UK market, Spectrum offers a range of IT services, including software development, consultancy, and project management, to clients across various industries.

  • 4. How are Westgate and Spectrum related?

    Westgate and Spectrum are strategic partners collaborating to bridge the gap between Indian IT companies and the UK IT sector. While Westgate focuses on facilitating access to UK IT projects for Indian firms, Spectrum leverages its established presence in the UK to support project execution and client management.

  • 5. What services does Westgate offer?

    Westgate offers a range of services including project identification, bid support, and partnership facilitation.

  • 6. What are public sector Projects?

    Public sector projects are government-funded initiatives. They include projects in areas such as healthcare, education, transportation, and social welfare.

  • 7. What are Private sector Projects?

    Private sector projects are initiatives undertaken by privately owned businesses or organizations for commercial purposes. These can include construction projects, product development, marketing campaigns, and other ventures aimed at generating profit in the competitive market.

  • 8. How can potential Suppliers get in touch with Westgate?

    Potential Suppliers can contact Westgate through the website, email, or phone to discuss their requirements and explore partnership opportunities.
