About Westgate

Westgate IT Hub Pvt. Ltd. Limited, is an innovative Bangalore based business in collaboration/partnership with Spectrum IT Hub Limited, Swindon, UK. We are proven, an emerging Marketplace and a partnership platform to facilitate UK IT Projects to execute by Indian IT Companies. 

Our aim is to

  • • Offer a One source system (OSS) access to IT project opportunities of UK, Europe, for our supplier partners to execute/take part of &
  • • Foster collaboration and partnerships with Indian IT firms looking for IT projects opportunities abroad

With a strong and capable network spanning at both private and public sectors of UK, we excel in connecting Indian firms with lucrative opportunities across the UK & we are leveraging our expertise and alliances to provide seamless pathways for Indian companies to participate in the competitive UK IT landscape.

Committed to transparency for our Suppliers (Your) success through offering personalised support from consultation to registration onto OSS to unlock the vast potential of the UK IT Sector market and grow your business to new heights of success.

About Spectrum IT Hub

Spectrum IT Hub Limited (SHU) is a strategic business based in UK and is alliance of three IT other companies originated from UK and working towards bidding and executing IT projects in UK public sector.

SHU is a business focused on building its strength through partnering with eligible IT firms of multiple domains/expertise from India. Hence SHU and Westgate IT Hub in India are partnered with each other to support the business objectives.
