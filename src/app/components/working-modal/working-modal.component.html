Working Models

To suit our suppliers and to fit the UK client demands, we are inviting our suppliers to choose their preferred models from below

1. Sub-contracting only: WestGate IT Hub Pvt Ltd and Spectrum IT Hub offer a sub-contracting solution to enable Indian IT companies to bid for and win UK public sector IT projects. This solution allows Indian IT companies to bid for UK public sector IT projects without needing to establish a presence in the UK. Spectrum IT Hub acts as the face for the Indian IT companies, handling the bidding process and submitting the project on behalf of the Indian IT companies. The Indian IT companies are then able to subcontract the project to their own team in India, ensuring that the project is completed to the highest standards.

2. Hybrid: Combining the benefits of subcontracting and direct involvement, our hybrid model offers suppliers the flexibility to choose their level of engagement, ensuring optimal alignment with project requirements and business objectives.  

3. On-site: In this model, our suppliers are directly involved in on-site project management and execution, providing hands-on support and expertise to ensure project success.

4. Resource Sharing: For recurring income through sharing the expert resources to UK for a specific project. Suppliers can choose this opportunity to contribute their expertise, establishing a lasting presence and generating ongoing revenue streams in the UK market.
